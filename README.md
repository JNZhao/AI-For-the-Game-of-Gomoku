# AI-For-the-Game-of-Gomoku
Implementing a simple AI engine for the game Gomoku, played on a 8 Ã— 8 board. In Gomoku, there are two players. One player plays with black stones and the other player plays with white stones. A player moves by placing a stone on an empty square on the board. The player who plays with black stones always moves first. After the first move, the players alternate. A player wins if she has placed five of her stones in a sequence, either horizontally, or vertically, or diagonally.
